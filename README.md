##  Project Overview
This project focuses on developing a complete brand identity for Wings & Go, a new fast-food restaurant aiming to compete in a crowded market. The main problem it solves is the lack of a strong, consistent visual identity that helps new food brands stand out and attract their target audience. Through strategic branding, logo design, packaging, social media visuals, and a launch campaign, the project builds a bold and cohesive brand presence. The final outcome will be presented as a full branding case study, showcasing the concept, visual system, mockups, and real-world applications across digital and print platforms.
